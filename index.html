Geographic Spread of Coronavirus

The interactive maps below illustrates the migaration of Coronavirus "COVID-19" worldwide

The map on the left represents the Hubs or the Origin of COVID-19 and all the destinations that were affected by the Hub country. The map on the right represents the countries the have received the cases Destinations. Each country may have one or more international source of COVID-19

Kindly note that cases from China were excluded, since China represents the main Hub of COVID-19

Hubs
Hover the mouse over one Hub to explore the migration of the COVID-19 cases to other countries

Destinations
Hover the mouse over one Destination to explore the sources of the COVID-19 cases

The data were manually collected from https://www.worldometers.info/coronavirus/

The data are up to March 17, 2019
